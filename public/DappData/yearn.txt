Yearn is a Decentralised finance application that finds optimum interest rates for
Ethereum based Assets from a number of providers and methods across the
Ethereum Blockchain.

The aim of this section is to remove the assets I have stored with Yearn and return
them to the Wallet and DApp Gateway.

Within this section of the document we will cover the following topics.

-= Connect to Yearn =-
-= Withdraw Assets process in Earn  =-
-= Withdraw Assets from Earn  =-
-= Withdraw Assets Process in Vaults  =-
-= Withdraw Assets from Vaults  =-

These may be referenced in other sections from other services in this document.
These topics may not be conducted in order and may need to be conducted multiple times
throughout this document.

-= Connect to Yearn =-

In your internet browser search/ address bar type: "yearn.finance/dashboard"

Press Enter to access the website.

Click "Connect your Wallet" on the left top right.

In the options select the Wallet identified in the Wallet and DApp Gateway section of
this document.

A new screen will pop-up asking for login-in details for your Wallet and DApp Gateway.

Log-in to you Wallet and DApp in the pop-up screen with your Wallet and DApp
Gateway password.

Select account with my Ethereum Address.

Click Connect.

If successful, on the Yearn page the "Connect your Wallet" button will be replaced with
my Ethereum Address.

-= Withdraw Assets process in Earn  =-

This section will outline how to withdraw a selection of assets back to your my wallet.

Note: The list of Assets/ Tokens to go through this process will be identified in the
next section, labelled -= Withdraw Assets from Earn  =-. Within this section it
will recommend what selections you need to make to withdraw each of the Assets.

In your internet browser search/ address bar type: "yearn.finance/earn"

Press Enter to access the website.

This page will display all the different types of Ethereum based Assets that can
be deposited on Yearn to earn interest on them.

There are options at the top of the page that will need to be to be selected,
this will be referenced as "Top Options" in this document.

The "Top Options" include:

- V1
- y.curve.fi
- busd.curve.fi

Once the correct "Top Option" has been selected you can search for the Asset that 
is to be withdrawn.

Click the Asset to be withdrawn. This will make the element larger.

On the right hand-side of the element click the "100%" text.

Click "Claim".

A new pop-up will be on the screen with the title "Confirm Transaction". 

You need to Confirm the transaction with the Wallet and DApp Gateway. As the Wallet
and DApp Gateway was connected previously another pop-up of the Wallet and DApp
Gateway will appear on the screen.

Click Confirm on the new Wallet and DApp Gateway pop-up.

The Transaction will be submitted to withdraw the Asset and it now needs to be
approved by the Ethereum Blockchain. Approval can take seconds, minutes, hours or days
depending on how many Transactions globally are awaiting approval.

The  Wallet and DApp Gateway will give confirmation when the Transaction has
been approved.

If the Transaction is rejected try the above steps again.

Once approved the Asset will now have been successfully transferred to the connected
Wallet and DApp Gateway.

Repeat the above steps for each to the Assets identified in
the -= Withdraw Assets from Earn  =- section.
